Deyue Zhou
PhD student / Travel enthusiast / Juvetini

Currently I am pursuing PhD degree in Chemistry and Materials at University College London after five-year professional experience in industry.

For work, I have academic-industrial and interdisciplinary backgrounds in polymers, nanomaterials and biosensors. I am interested in nano-synthesis, surface technology, molecular assembly and optical sensing. The ultimate goal of my project is to promote efficient and reliable nanobiosensing in diagnosis, pharmacology, environment monitor and other interesting fields. To comply with the challenge, I have been equipped myself with advanced characterization technique and proficiency in data analysis.

For life, I am juggling between quite a few hobbies. Football is a huge part of my life, and Juventus is the eternal love. I love travelling, particularly I treat myself as a “frequent flyer” with decent expert and experience in world-class airlines and hotels. Coffee is my daily essential, but black. I’ve travelled around the world visiting fabulous coffees, which of course doesn’t count London. I love wine, especially with firends. I live in London at present, if you want to find me for a glass.

More fortunately, I have my sweet family, my adorable angel, and her.
